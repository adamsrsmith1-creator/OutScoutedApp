What We Track

Every Edge.
On One Report.

OutScouted delivers deep, actionable intelligence on your upcoming opponents — so you walk into every game with a real advantage.

Pitcher Velocity & Tendencies

Pitch-by-pitch data including average velocity, pitch mix, release points, and zone tendencies. Know exactly what's coming before it leaves the hand.

Hitter Spray Charts

Visual spray charts for every hitter in their lineup. Identify pull tendencies, contact zones, and strikeout patterns to set your defense and attack the zone.

Full Lineup Reports

Complete game-ready breakdowns on the entire opposing lineup. From leadoff through the bottom of the order — fully profiled and ready the night before.

Exit Velocity & Launch Angle

Hard-hit rate and exit velocity tracked for power hitters. Understand who presents the real threat and position your outfield accordingly.

Season-Long Trends

Track opponent performance across an entire season. See how they adjust, how pitchers fatigue, and how lineups shift as the postseason approaches.

Self-Scouting Reports

Turn the lens inward. Get access to reports on your own players — identify weaknesses before opponents do and build a development plan all season long.
